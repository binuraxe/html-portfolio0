The best movies I have ever seen

Top 3

Teacher, I really recommend you to watch these films. I even cried during the last one

Mr. & Mrs. Smith (2005)

John and Jane Smith seem like an ordinary married couple, but both are secretly assassins working for rival organizations. Their lives take a turn when they are assigned to eliminate each other.

Slumdog Millionaire (2008)

Jamal Malik, a boy from the slums of Mumbai, competes in "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?", raising suspicions about how he knows the answers. Each question is linked to a moment from his past, revealing his life story.

The Pursuit of Happyness (2006)

Chris Gardner, a struggling single father, faces homelessness while trying to build a better future for his son. Despite the hardships, he takes on an unpaid internship at a brokerage firm, hoping for a chance at success.
